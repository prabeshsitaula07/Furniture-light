Frequently Asked Question
1 What types of furniture do you offer on your website?
We offer a wide range of furniture options to cater to various tastes and needs. Our collection includes sofas, chairs, tables, beds, cabinets, shelves, and more. Whether you're looking for modern, traditional, minimalist, or eclectic styles, we have furniture pieces to suit every interior design preference.
2 How can I place an order for furniture on your website?
Placing an order on our website is easy and convenient. Simply browse through our furniture categories, select the items you wish to purchase, and add them to your cart. Once you have finished selecting your furniture pieces, proceed to the checkout page where you can review your order details and make any necessary adjustments. Finally, enter your shipping and payment information to complete the order process.
3 Do you offer customization options for your furniture?
Yes, we understand that each customer may have unique preferences and requirements for their furniture. That's why we offer customization options for many of our furniture pieces. You can often choose from a range of upholstery materials, finishes, colors, and sizes to personalize your furniture according to your specific needs. Look for the "Customize" or "Personalize" options on the product pages for more details.
4 What is your return policy for furniture purchased from your website?
We want to ensure your complete satisfaction with your furniture purchase. If, for any reason, you are not satisfied with your order, we offer a hassle-free return policy. You may initiate a return within 30 days of receiving the furniture. Please note that certain items may be subject to a restocking fee. For detailed information on our return process, eligibility, and any associated costs, please refer to our Returns and Refunds page or contact our customer support team for assistance.
5 Do you offer assembly services for the furniture?
Yes, we offer assembly services for select furniture items. You can find information about this service on the product page or contact our customer service for more details.

Yes, once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number that you can use to monitor the progress of your delivery. You can usually track it on our website or through the shipping carrier's website.

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and sometimes alternative options like Apple Pay or Google Pay. The available payment methods are typically listed during the checkout process.
